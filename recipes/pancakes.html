Pancakes

Description

Mix 200 g flour, 2 eggs, 300 ml milk, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt until smooth. Heat a non-stick pan with a little oil or butter. Pour a ladle of batter, cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side until golden. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve warm with syrup, honey, or fruit.

Ingredients

  • 200 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Syrup, honey, or fruit (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs, milk, sugar, baking powder, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan with butter or oil.
  3. Pour a ladle of batter, cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  4. Cook the other side until golden.
  5. Serve warm with syrup, honey, or fruit.
